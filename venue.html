Venue!

The Mountain Terrace is at the top of a hill in Woodside, surrounded by redwoods and with small glimpses of the bay.

Our ceremony will be outdoors on grass, so flats or wedges are recommended. Cocktails and dinner will also be outside with a dance floor and bar located inside the lodge. We're excited about the firepit with S'mores, so layers are recommended because the night will likely be a bit chilly.

Steph will be wearing a wedding dress and Thomas will be wearing a tuxdeo, but please wear what you feel comfortable in. We are coining the dress style as fun formal.
